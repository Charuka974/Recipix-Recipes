11. Fresh Lemonade üçã

Ingredients:

  1. 6 large lemons (or 1 cup fresh lemon juice)
  2. 1 cup sugar (or to taste)
  3. 5 cups cold water
  4. Ice cubes
  5. 1 lemon, sliced (for garnish)
  6. Mint leaves (optional, for garnish)

Instructions:

Juice Lemons: Cut lemons in half and juice them until you have about 1 cup of lemon juice.
Make Simple Syrup: In a small saucepan, combine 1 cup of water and the sugar. Heat over medium heat, stirring until the sugar dissolves completely. Remove from heat and let cool.
Mix Lemonade: In a large pitcher, combine the lemon juice, simple syrup, and remaining 4 cups of cold water. Stir well to combine.
Adjust Sweetness: Taste the lemonade and adjust sweetness by adding more sugar or water as needed.
Serve: Fill glasses with ice cubes and pour lemonade over. Garnish with lemon slices and mint leaves if desired.
