18. Meringue Cookies üç™

Ingredients:

  1. 4 large egg whites (at room temperature)
  2. 1 cup granulated sugar
  3. 1 tsp vanilla extract
  4. 1/4 tsp cream of tartar
  5. Pinch of salt

Instructions:

Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 225¬∞F (107¬∞C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
Whisk Egg Whites: In a clean, dry bowl, beat the egg whites using an electric mixer on medium speed until frothy. Add the cream of tartar and a pinch of salt, and continue beating until soft peaks form.
Add Sugar Gradually: Slowly add sugar, 1 tablespoon at a time, while continuing to beat on high speed. Once all the sugar is added, keep beating for about 5 minutes, or until stiff, glossy peaks form. You should be able to hold the bowl upside down without the meringue falling out.
Add Vanilla: Mix in the vanilla extract.
Pipe or Spoon Meringue: Using a piping bag or a spoon, dollop or pipe the meringue mixture onto the prepared baking sheet, forming small, round mounds or other desired shapes. Leave some space between each cookie.
Bake: Bake in the preheated oven for 1 to 1.5 hours, or until the meringue cookies are dry to the touch and can easily be lifted from the parchment paper. The cookies should be white and crisp on the outside.
Cool: Turn off the oven and leave the meringues in the oven with the door slightly ajar for an additional 30 minutes to cool completely and crisp up.
Serve: Once cool, enjoy your light and airy meringue cookies, or store them in an airtight container for up to a week.
