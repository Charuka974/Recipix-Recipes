Ingredients:

For the Boba Pearls:

  1. 1 cup tapioca pearls
  2. 5 cups water
  3. Â½ cup brown sugar (or honey/sugar syrup)

For the Tea:

  1. 2 black tea bags (or green tea for variation)
  2. 2 cups hot water
  3. 2 tbsp sugar (optional, adjust to taste)

For the Milk or Alternative:

  1. 1 cup milk (dairy or plant-based like almond, oat, or coconut milk)
  2. Ice cubes

Instructions:

Cook Boba Pearls: Bring 5 cups of water to a boil. Add the tapioca pearls and cook according to package instructions (typically 20-30 minutes). Stir occasionally to prevent sticking.
Sweeten the Boba: Once the pearls are cooked, drain them and transfer them into a bowl. Add the brown sugar (or honey) and mix well until the pearls are coated in the syrup.
Brew the Tea: In a separate container, steep the tea bags in 2 cups of hot water for 5 minutes. Remove the tea bags and add sugar if desired. Let it cool to room temperature or chill in the fridge for a faster option.
Assemble the Drink: In a glass, add a handful of ice cubes. Pour in the cooled tea, then add about 2-3 tablespoons of sweetened boba pearls.
Add Milk: Pour milk over the ice and tea mixture. Stir well to combine.
Serve: Mix and enjoy your homemade bubble tea with a large straw, and serve immediately!
