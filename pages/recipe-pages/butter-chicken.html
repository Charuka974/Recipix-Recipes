Ingredients:

  1. 500g boneless chicken, cut into cubes
  2. 1 cup yogurt
  3. 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  4. 1 tsp garam masala
  5. Â½ tsp turmeric
  6. 1 tsp chili powder
  7. 1 tbsp oil
  8. 2 tbsp butter
  9. 1 cup tomato puree
  10. Â½ cup heavy cream
  11. 1 tsp sugar
  12. Salt to taste

Instructions:

Marinate Chicken: Mix chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, and spices. Marinate for at least 1 hour.
Cook Chicken: Heat oil in a pan, cook chicken until golden brown, and set aside.
Prepare Sauce: In the same pan, melt butter, add tomato puree, and cook for 5 minutes. Add sugar and salt.
Combine: Add chicken back, stir in cream, and simmer for 10 minutes.
Serve: Garnish with cilantro and serve with rice or naan.
