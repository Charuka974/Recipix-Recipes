13. Homemade Ramen üçú

Ingredients:

For the Broth:

  1. 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  2. 2 cups water
  3. 1 tbsp soy sauce
  4. 1 tbsp miso paste (optional for richer flavor)
  5. 1 tsp sesame oil
  6. 2 cloves garlic, minced
  7. 1-inch ginger, sliced
  8. 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  9. 1 tsp chili paste (optional for heat)

For the Ramen:

  1. 2 servings ramen noodles (fresh or dried)
  2. 2 soft-boiled eggs
  3. 2 green onions, sliced
  4. 1 cup cooked chicken or pork (optional, thinly sliced)
  5. 1/2 cup corn kernels (optional)
  6. 1/2 cup spinach or bok choy (optional)
  7. 1 sheet nori (seaweed, optional)
  8. Sesame seeds (optional, for garnish)

Instructions:

Prepare Broth: In a large pot, combine chicken broth, water, soy sauce, miso paste, sesame oil, garlic, ginger, rice vinegar, and chili paste. Bring to a simmer over medium heat and cook for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally. Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.
Cook Noodles: In a separate pot, cook the ramen noodles according to package instructions (about 3-5 minutes). Drain and set aside.
Soft-Boil Eggs: Bring water to a boil in a small pot, gently add eggs and cook for 6-7 minutes for soft-boiled eggs. Transfer eggs to an ice bath to cool, peel, and set aside.
Assemble Ramen: Divide cooked noodles between two bowls. Pour hot broth over the noodles, ensuring they are submerged.
Add Toppings: Top the ramen with sliced soft-boiled eggs, green onions, cooked chicken or pork, corn, spinach, and any additional toppings you prefer.
Garnish: Add a sheet of nori (seaweed) and sprinkle with sesame seeds before serving.
Serve: Enjoy your homemade ramen hot and fresh!

üëâ Pro Tip: For extra flavor, marinate your chicken or pork in soy sauce and garlic before cooking!
