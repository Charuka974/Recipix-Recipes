27. Iced Matcha Latte üçµ

Ingredients:

  1. 2 tsp matcha green tea powder
  2. 2 tbsp hot water
  3. 1 cup milk (dairy or plant-based)
  4. Ice cubes
  5. Sweetener to taste (e.g., honey, syrup)

Instructions:

Whisk matcha powder with hot water until fully dissolved.
Fill a glass with ice and pour in the milk.
Add the matcha mixture and stir well.
Sweeten to taste. Enjoy cold!
