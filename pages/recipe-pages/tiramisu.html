20. Classic Tiramisu üçÆ

Ingredients:

For the Cream Layer:

  1. 250g mascarpone cheese
  2. 2 large eggs (separate yolks and whites)
  3. 3 tbsp sugar
  4. 1 tsp vanilla extract
  5. 1 cup heavy cream

For the Coffee Layer:

  1. 1 ¬Ω cups strong brewed coffee (cooled)
  2. 2 tbsp coffee liqueur (optional, like Kahl√∫a)

For the Assembly:

  1. 24 ladyfingers (savoiardi)
  2. Cocoa powder, for dusting

Instructions:

Prepare the Cream: In a large mixing bowl, beat egg yolks with sugar and vanilla until smooth. Add mascarpone cheese and mix until well combined. In a separate bowl, whip heavy cream to stiff peaks and gently fold it into the mascarpone mixture.
Whisk the Egg Whites: In another bowl, beat egg whites until soft peaks form. Gently fold the whipped egg whites into the mascarpone mixture to lighten it.
Prepare the Coffee: Mix the brewed coffee with coffee liqueur (if using).
Assemble the Tiramisu: Quickly dip each ladyfinger into the coffee mixture (don't soak them too long). Arrange a layer of dipped ladyfingers at the bottom of a 9x9-inch dish. Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers. Repeat the layers with the remaining ladyfingers and mascarpone mixture.
Chill: Refrigerate the tiramisu for at least 4 hours, or preferably overnight, to let the flavors meld.
Dust with Cocoa Powder: Before serving, dust the top with a generous layer of cocoa powder using a fine sieve.
Serve: Slice and enjoy your delicious homemade tiramisu!

üëâ Pro Tip: For a richer flavor, let the tiramisu sit in the fridge overnight for the best texture!
