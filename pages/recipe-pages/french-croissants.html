04. Homemade French Croissants ü•ê

Ingredients:

  1. 4 cups all-purpose flour
  2. 1 tbsp sugar
  3. 2 tsp salt
  4. 1 packet (7g) active dry yeast
  5. 1 cup warm milk
  6. 250g unsalted butter, cold
  7. 1 egg (for egg wash)

Instructions:

Activate Yeast: Mix yeast with warm milk and sugar. Let it sit until foamy (5-10 minutes).
Make Dough: Mix flour and salt in a bowl. Add yeast mixture and knead until smooth. Refrigerate for 2 hours.
Prepare Butter: Flatten cold butter into a square between parchment paper.
Laminate Dough: Roll out dough, place butter in the center, fold, and roll again. Repeat 3 times with chilling in between.
Shape Croissants: Cut triangles, roll them into crescent shapes, and let rise for 1 hour.
Bake: Brush with egg wash and bake at 200¬∞C (400¬∞F) for 15-20 minutes.
