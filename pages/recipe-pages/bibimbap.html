21. Bibimbap üçö

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably short-grain white rice)
  • 1 cup spinach, blanched
  • 1 small zucchini, julienned
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 100g shiitake mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 eggs (fried sunny-side up)
  • 1/4 cup bean sprouts, blanched
  • 1/4 cup kimchi (optional)
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp gochujang (Korean chili paste)
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds
  • 1 tsp garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp rice vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

Prepare Vegetables: Saut√© zucchini, carrots, and shiitake mushrooms in a little sesame oil over medium heat. Season each vegetable with a pinch of salt and set aside.
Prepare Spinach: Blanch the spinach in boiling water for 1-2 minutes, then drain and squeeze out excess water. Season with a little sesame oil, minced garlic, and salt.
Prepare Bean Sprouts: Blanch the bean sprouts in boiling water for 2-3 minutes, then drain and season with a little sesame oil and salt.
Prepare Rice: In a bowl, place the cooked rice as the base. Drizzle a little sesame oil over it to add flavor.
Fry Eggs: In a separate pan, fry eggs sunny-side up, keeping the yolks intact.
Assemble Bibimbap: Arrange each prepared vegetable (spinach, zucchini, carrots, mushrooms, bean sprouts, kimchi) neatly on top of the rice. Place the fried egg in the center.
Add Sauce: In a small bowl, mix gochujang, soy sauce, rice vinegar, garlic, sesame seeds, and sugar. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper to taste.
Serve: Drizzle the sauce over the rice and mix everything together before eating. Enjoy your bibimbap hot!

üëâ Pro Tip: For extra flavor, drizzle more sesame oil on top just before serving!
