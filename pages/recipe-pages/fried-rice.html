14. Fried Rice üçö

Ingredients:

  1. 2 cups cooked rice (preferably a day old)
  2. 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  3. 2 cloves garlic, minced
  4. 1 small onion, finely chopped
  5. 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn, etc.)
  6. 2 large eggs, beaten
  7. 3 tbsp soy sauce
  8. 1 tsp sesame oil (optional)
  9. 1 tsp ginger, minced (optional)
  10. 1-2 green onions, chopped (for garnish)
  11. Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

Prepare Rice: If using fresh rice, spread it out on a tray to cool down and let the moisture evaporate. Day-old rice works best for fried rice because it‚Äôs drier and separates more easily.
Heat Oil: In a large wok or skillet, heat vegetable oil over medium-high heat.
Saut√© Aromatics: Add garlic, onion, and ginger (if using), saut√©ing for 2-3 minutes until fragrant and softened.
Add Veggies: Add mixed vegetables and cook for 2-3 minutes until tender.
Scramble Eggs: Push the veggies to the side of the pan. Pour the beaten eggs into the empty side of the pan and scramble them until cooked.
Stir in Rice: Add the cooked rice to the pan and stir to combine everything.
Season: Add soy sauce, sesame oil (if using), and season with salt and pepper to taste. Stir everything together and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
Garnish and Serve: Garnish with chopped green onions, and serve hot!
