6️⃣ Strawberry Cheesecake 🍓

Ingredients:

For the Crust:

  1. 200g graham crackers, crushed
  2. 5 tbsp melted butter
  3. 2 tbsp sugar

For the Filling:

  1. 500g cream cheese
  2. 1 cup sugar
  3. 2 eggs
  4. 1 tsp vanilla extract
  5. ½ cup sour cream

For the Topping:

  1. 1 cup fresh strawberries, sliced
  2. ¼ cup strawberry jam

Instructions:

Prepare Crust: Mix crushed crackers with melted butter and sugar. Press into a baking pan.
Make Filling: Beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth. Add eggs, vanilla, and sour cream.
Bake: Pour over crust and bake at 160°C (325°F) for 45 minutes.
Chill: Let cool and refrigerate for 4 hours.
Add Toppings: Spread jam over the cheesecake and top with fresh strawberries.

👉 Pro Tip: Use a water bath while baking for a creamy texture!
