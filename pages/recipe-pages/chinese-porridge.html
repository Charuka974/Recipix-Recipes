08. Chinese Porridge (Congee) üç≤

Ingredients:

For the Congee Base:

  1. 1 cup jasmine rice (or short-grain rice)
  2. 6 cups water or chicken broth (for extra flavor)
  3. 1 tbsp ginger, finely chopped
  4. 1-2 tbsp soy sauce (optional)
  5. Salt to taste

For the Toppings (Optional):

  1. 2 boiled eggs, peeled and halved
  2. 2 tbsp pickled vegetables (such as pickled radish or mustard greens)
  3. 1 tbsp sesame oil
  4. 1 green onion, thinly sliced
  5. 1 tbsp cilantro, chopped
  6. Fried shallots or garlic (optional)
  7. Chili oil (for a spicy kick)

Instructions:

Rinse Rice: Wash the rice thoroughly under cold water until the water runs clear.
Cook Rice: In a large pot, combine rice, water or chicken broth, and ginger. Bring to a boil.
Simmer: Reduce the heat to low and let the mixture simmer, uncovered, for 45-60 minutes, stirring occasionally. The rice should break down and become soft and porridge-like. Add more water or broth if needed to reach the desired consistency.
Season: Add soy sauce (if using) and salt to taste.
Serve: Once the porridge reaches a creamy texture, serve it in bowls.
Top with Toppings: Add your choice of toppings like boiled eggs, pickled vegetables, sesame oil, and fresh herbs. Drizzle with chili oil for spice (optional).

üëâ Pro Tip: For a smoother texture, cook the congee longer, and stir occasionally!
