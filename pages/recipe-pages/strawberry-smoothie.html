25. Strawberry Smoothie üçì

Ingredients:

  1. 1 cup strawberries
  2. 1/2 banana
  3. 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  4. 1/2 cup milk or juice
  5. 1 tsp honey (optional)
  6. Ice cubes (if using fresh fruit)

Instructions:

Combine all ingredients in a blender.
Blend until smooth and creamy.
Pour into a glass and serve immediately.
